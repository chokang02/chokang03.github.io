It is one of the classic albums that symbolizes Korea's second-generation indie scene, and is still considered Black Skirts' best work, even though Black Skirts has released five full-length albums. Overall, it is based on the rough punk rock of the British and American camps, and is an album that shows exotic yet sophisticated musicality by mixing the melodic guitar pop of the Strokes or Suede with the soft modern rock sensibility of the Korean indie scene. With this album, Black Skirt was nominated in five categories at the 2010 Korean Music Awards, winning the Best Modern Rock Album award.
